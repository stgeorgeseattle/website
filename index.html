Father Angelos Sarkis
Hegumen


frangelossarkis@stgeorgeseattle.org



+1 (949) 514 8488

Father Angelos Sarkis was born on May 23rd, 1960. He was raised in Tanta, Egypt, where he received a Bachelor of Science in Business and Accounting in 1982. Fr. Angelos also received a Bachelor in Theology at the Clerical College in Cairo, Egypt in 1993.

He was ordained a Presbyter by His Grace Bishop Paula, on March 3rd, 1995, over St. George Cathedral in Tanta, Egypt. He served in Tanta for 8 years as a priest, after which he was chosen by His Holiness Pope Shenouda III to serve the congregation of St. George church in Seattle, WA on April 15th, 2003. Fr. Angelos was elevated to Hegumen on October 10, 2018 by the hands of His Holiness Pope Tawadros II.

Communities Served

This effort is to involve our children and grow the spirit of service within them, through serving Christ in the poor and those in need. If you want to contribute please email: goodsamaritans@stgeorgeseattle.org

Saint Abraham

Newcomers

Saint Abraham service is for newcomers and immigrants families and individuals relocating to the Seattle area. The service goal is to show Christ's love to people starting a new life in Seattle. We provide relocation support as needed, including advice on housing, schools, support in filing for employment applications and a driver's license.

Unfortunately we do not have any employment opportunities at Church or temporary housing.
